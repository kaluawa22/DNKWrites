Déjà King About Me

Déjà King is a self-taught creative artist with a passion for art focused on vibrant colors, strong features and faces, and grandiose scenic backgrounds. Focused on bringing to canvas the colors, sounds, tastes, and feelings that capture the energies of real life --- DNK designs and writings are meant to take their viewer into the mind of the artist.

Most of all, as a Black woman, capturing the culture and energy of the black experience is central to DNK artistic themes.
